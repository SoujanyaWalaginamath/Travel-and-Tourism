DAL LAKE

Dal Lake, also known as the "Jewel of Srinagar", is a majestic and picturesque lake located in the heart of Srinagar, Kashmir. The lake is surrounded by majestic mountains and lush greenery, and is home to a vibrant floating market, where locals sell everything from fresh flowers to delicious Kashmiri delicacies. The lake's tranquil waters reflect the beauty of the surrounding landscape, making it a popular destination for boating, fishing, and relaxation. Whether you're looking for adventure, romance, or simply a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, Dal Lake has something to offer everyone.
